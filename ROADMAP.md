# Loadlyx roadmap

Store, quote system, auto-generated loads, Stripe checkout, analytics, SEO, and admin dashboard.

## Phase 1.5
Public load feed, load detail pages, carrier signup placeholder, and carrier-interest capture.

## Phase 2
Mover lead marketplace, carrier profiles, carrier dashboard, lead unlock flow, and marketplace monetization.

## Phase 3
Carrier matching engine, carrier bidding, dispatch automation, and Stripe Connect payouts.

## Phase 4
Embedded quote widget network, partner referrals, and affiliate payouts.
